About

The "About" section offers insight into ABBA's mission, history, and the communities we serve. Learn how your contributions aid our efforts in Afghanistan.

Our Mission

ABBA works to provide the most vulnerable in Afghanistan with equitable access to food, housing, technology, education, and monetary funds. Our mission is to get Afghanistan back on track to being a developed country. We are here for the sole purpose of helping Afghanistan advance in terms of education and poverty.

Our History

ABBA is a start-up NGO hoping to collect humanitarian and tech donations to reach disadvantaged populations throughout various provinces in Afghanistan. We are a brand-new organization that is looking to collect technologies along with donations for the children and the needy.

Who We Serve

Our overall goal is to expand throughout the whole of Afghanistan, but as of now, our population consists of families in certain provinces (Ghazni and Paktika) and districts. ABBA will begin to operate and distribute resources in the provinces of Ghazni and Paktika, then eventually expand to build and strengthen other districts confronted with the devastating aftermaths of war.

How Donations Are Used

If you choose to donate, your money will go towards acquiring and distributing technological and humanitarian assistance to the men, women, and children of Afghanistan. The funding will help aid sustainable economic development and education.

Collections

ABBA is a start-up NGO that collects mobile, tablet, and laptop devices to support education and communication in Afghanistan. We aim to alleviate poverty and advance the country towards development.
